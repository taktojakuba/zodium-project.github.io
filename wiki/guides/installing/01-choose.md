## Overview

This guide walks you through installing a Zodium-Project Distro on a bare machine using a bootable ISO & USB drive

## What you'll need

- A USB drive (8GB or larger)
- ISO downloaded from the wiki (or get-started)
- A machine with UEFI firmware
- Linux Supported Hardware (most hardware should just work)

``
Note: Microsoft Surface & Macbook T2 laptops have limited support on linux , we dont gurantee support for these laptops  
``

``
Note: Some Printers & Pherepherals (caputre-cards,mics,radom tech gadgets) might  have limited or no support on linux , you are recommended to verify if all you stuff supports linux
``

You can download ISO of your desired distro from here : https://zodium-project.github.io/get-started

## Hardware requirements
- 8GB RAM (or above) is recommended , but 4GB RAM can work
- Any decent intel/amd cpu from last 10-12 years should work
- SSD's are recommended for a much better experience
- 10-20GB Free Space is recommended at minimum 

`
TPM-2.0 & Secure Boot is only required if you want to use Luks2-TPM2-auto-decryption (most of you are not gonna use it)
`

## What you are expected to do/know
- how to enter Boot-Menu/BIOS in your device
- You should be able to read stuff 
- Follow this guide properly
